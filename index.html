The Online Algorithmic Complexity Calculator

v2.0

 

 

This calculator represents a new approach and provides improved estimations of Kolmogorov-Chaitin complexity ($K$) of strings and 2-dimensional arrays. It uses a method ($BDM$) based upon Algorithmic Probability, which is compatible with (but beyond the scope of) lossless compression algorithms whose implementations are based on calculations of Shannon entropy ($S$) (e.g. Lempel-Ziv, Lempel-Ziv-Welch, DEFLATE, etc). Such algorithms have been widely used to estimate $K$. In contrast to compression methods, $BDM$ not only considers statistical regularities but is also sensitive to segments of algorithmic nature, such as in a sequence like $12345...$ which $S$ and lossless compression algorithms would characterize as having maximum randomness and the highest degree of incompressibility despite its recursive nature. For $BDM$, the same sequence would have a low algorithmic complexity, as there are many short computer programs that generate it.
